Wobbly is a workplace organising platform being designed and prototyped by a small voluntary team of developers. It’s a communication and coordination tool with structures and processes modelled on the IWW’s organising style (hence the name, coming from the IWW nickname). We’re creating a space for energetic, powerful, and democratic unions to win struggles and grow.

If you'd like more info, visit our Wiki page or you can subscribe to our mailing list. If you're interested in contributing, please fill in the form below and we'll be in touch as soon as possible.
